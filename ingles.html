Characteristic


“La Estancia de Runtún” It is in the middle of nature, surrounded by imposing mountain landscapes and is located in the epicenter of different tourist attractions. Ideal for families, groups of young people with an adventurous spirit.


Installations


Upon arriving at “La Estancia de Runtún” You will have access to large green spaces. A shared kitchen, where you can prepare and bring food that you are going to consume. The accommodation has a WIFI zone, security cameras, grill, kitchen parking fully equipped (refrigerator, 2 kitchenettes, microwave, crockery, 2 sandwich makers).


optional breakfast


$3/ person, includes cheese sandwich, milk or tea, jam, juice and eggs.


How to get to “La Estancia de Runtún”


“La Estancia de Runtún” It is located at Km 7 of the Vía a Runtún, 20 minutes from the city of Baños de Agua Santa. the same route by which you climb to the Tree House. As a reference is "La Resbaladera" (we are 1 km above).


Transport Via Runtún Km 7


“La Estancia de Runtún” It is located via Runtún at Km 7, the same road that leads to the Tree House. If you don't come by car, There is a taxi service and rental vans in Baños. As for buses, the bus stop in Baños is in Pastaza Street and Vicente Rocafuerte. The departure times from the stop to Runtún (Casa del Árbol) are: 6:00 am, 11:00 am, 2:00 pm and 4:00 pm. *Be at the stop 20 minutes before the indicated hours*. The schedules of the buses from Runtún to Baños are: 8:00 am, 1:00 pm, 4:00 pm and 6:00 pm. The transportation services described above are not included in the price.

Visit Baños

Baños de Agua Santa is a city in the province of Tungurahua in Ecuador and is an access road to the Amazon basin. It is known for its access to trails on the active volcano Tungurahua to the south and for its hot springs rich in minerals. In the center, the Church of Our Lady of the Holy Water has paintings representing miracles attributed to the Virgin Mary. To the east, El Pailón del Diablo and Agoyán are spectacular waterfalls on the Pastaza River.

Activities you can do

“La Estancia de Runtún” It is located in the epicenter of different tourist attractions such as the Tree House, theme parks, trails, giant swings and orchid gardens. After venturing into nature you can return to rest in our Estancia.
